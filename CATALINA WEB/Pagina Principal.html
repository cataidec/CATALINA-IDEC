Welcome to our website. We're a Christian institution called Catalina Landis

Christian Bilingual Preschool & School. God placed the need for an evangelical

school in the Church of God is Love of San Pedro Sula. In 1972 classes began

at the Catalina Landis School. The idea behind ​​the name of the institution is

because Pastor Marriaga wanted to honor the memory of a missionary who

showed great love, sacrifice and dedication for the children of Honduras.
